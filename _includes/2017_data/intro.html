About the Dialogue

A high-level policy dialogue convening government and industry leaders across APEC economies to examine the role of artificial intelligence in shaping the future of digital trade.

DATE
April 22, 2026
TIME
12:00 PM CST
LOCATION
Mexico City, Mexico

Since 2016, APEC Digital Trade Policy Dialogues have provided a platform for policymakers and industry leaders to engage on emerging trends and shared challenges in the digital economy. The 2026 Dialogue builds on this foundation by examining how artificial intelligence is shaping digital trade and what is needed to support industry-led innovation.

The dialogue will focus on key policy areas including cross-border data flows, regulatory interoperability, and cybersecurity, and will highlight the importance of public-private collaboration in advancing AI innovation across APEC economies.

  • • AI’s role in shaping digital trade policy
  • • Enabling frameworks for cross-border data flows and innovation
  • • Public-private collaboration to strengthen AI ecosystems
