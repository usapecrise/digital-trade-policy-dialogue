Background

The U.S. Department of State’s Bureau of Cyberspace and Digital Policy (CDP), through the Technology Gateway (TECHGATE) Program, is convening a private sector roundtable to explore pathways for accelerated adoption of trusted U.S. digital solutions in strategic global markets.

Implemented by Cadmus, TECHGATE supports partner governments in advancing secure, reliable digital ecosystems while strengthening engagement with trusted U.S. technology providers.

Focus

The roundtable will examine commercial opportunities, market-entry barriers, deployment risks, and partnership models that can support secure technology adoption globally.

Discussions will also explore how TECHGATE can help facilitate engagement between U.S. technology firms and partner governments through strategic advising, market coordination, and risk mitigation support.

Audience

Leaders and representatives from U.S. technology firms engaged in cybersecurity, AI enablement, connectivity infrastructure, cloud services, digital policy, and international market expansion.
