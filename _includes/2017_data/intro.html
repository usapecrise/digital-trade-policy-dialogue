Background

The U.S. Department of State’s Bureau of Cyberspace and Digital Policy (CDP), through the Technology Gateway (TECHGATE) Program, is convening a private sector roundtable to explore opportunities for commercial engagement in digital infrastructure, cybersecurity, connectivity, and AI enablement. Implemented by the Cadmus Group, TECHGATE supports foreign governments in adopting secure, reliable digital technologies while strengthening partnerships with trusted U.S. technology firms in strategic and emerging markets.

Focus

This roundtable will examine commercial opportunities, market-entry barriers, operational risks, and partnership models that can support trusted technology adoption globally. Discussions will also explore how TECHGATE can help facilitate engagement between U.S. technology firms and partner governments through strategic advising, ecosystem development, and risk mitigation support.

Audience

Representatives from U.S. technology firms with expertise in cybersecurity, AI enablement, connectivity infrastructure, cloud services, digital policy, and international market engagement.
