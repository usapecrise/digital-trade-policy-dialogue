Background

The United States will hold a Digital Trade Policy Dialogue (TPD) on the margins of the Second ABAC Meeting (ABAC 2) in Mexico City, Mexico on Wednesday, April 22, 2026. This policy dialogue will explore how AI is shaping digital trade and what is needed to support industry-led AI innovation. The dialogue will also highlight industry leadership and best practices, emphasizing how governments, businesses, and other stakeholders can work together to strengthen AI innovation. Since 2016, TPDs have provided a platform for policymakers and industry leaders to discuss emerging trends and challenges in the digital economy. As economies increasingly utilize AI to drive innovation and economic growth, APEC continues to build on the goals set out in the APEC AI Initiative and the 2025 APEC Digital and AI Ministerial Statement.

Focus

The 2026 Dialogue examines how artificial intelligence is shaping digital trade and what is needed to support industry-led innovation.

Audience

Policymakers, industry leaders, and digital trade practitioners across APEC economies.
